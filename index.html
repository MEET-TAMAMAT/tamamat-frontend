How it Works

Teach Faster with Smart Shortcuts

Manual Selection

  • Students are automatically assigned keyboard numbers from [1] to [9]. The button [5] mutes/unmutes all
  • The teacher presses a student's number to unmute and highlight them
  • A student is ready to answer

Auto Selection

  • Press [0] to randomly select a student
  • The system instantly chooses one participant, unmutes them, and highlights them on the screen
  • Each press generates a new random selection

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Pay-as-You-Go option mean?

It means you only pay when you use it. No monthly or subscription fees. Pricing is based on a per-participant, per-minute model.

How does the Pay-as-You-Go plan calculate the cost?

The platform charges $0.005 per minute per participant. For example, if you have a full class of eight students, plus the teacher and screen sharing, there are 10 participants in total. A one-hour lesson would cost about $3 (60 minutes x 10 participants x $0.005). The fewer students you have and the shorter the lesson, the less you pay. Nice and simple.

Do teachers or students need to install any software, and what devices can they use?

No installation is required. Teachers can simply use a web browser on their computer (PC, Mac, or Linux). Students can join from a computer as well, or even use their iPhone or Android phone.

How does the participation system work?

Each student is assigned a unique number button on the numeric keyboard, so you can either manually or automatically call up students instead of calling them by their names.

Can teachers manage their students inside the platform?

Yes. Teachers can easily add or remove students, send invitations, add notes, and view class statistics.

How do students join my classroom?

Teachers can send email invitations to students. All you need is the student's valid email address.

Do my students need to create an account?

After students receive a class invitation from the teacher, they can either log in with their Gmail account or register using their own email and password.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is securely protected using SSL encryption.

What happens if a student loses connection?

If a student loses connection, they can rejoin the classroom anytime by refreshing the page or clicking the classroom link again.
